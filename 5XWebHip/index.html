Our Process

How We Bring Your Idea to Market
1

Plan & Develop

We research and find the best way to build your idea. We plan a comprehensive approach will help define the problem, viability of the solution, and cost benefit for stakeholders. Next is the design process: simple, elegant solutions that are easy to use and easy to maintain is our focus. When it comes time to develop, our expert development team can deliver a high quality product, on time, with excellent results.

2

Deploy

Because execution is so crucial, we put a lot of effort and careful consideration into our approach. A complete deployment strategy insures your launch will be a success. This is what makes 5X Solutions different from it's competitors, the thought and care of your idea after the initial development process.

3

Manage

At 5X Solutions, we want your idea to succeed. That's why we want to ensure that after it is launched, your product stays successful and your vision is achieved. Our post production management and maintenance strategies will enhance the usability and life of the product.
